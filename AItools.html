Key guidance for using the assistants

  • Sign in with a free or existing Google, Microsoft, Apple, or Gmail account to use these assistants.
  • Provide clear context: name the unit for discussion reviews or upload your project deck to receive targeted feedback.
  • If a response seems generic, prompt the assistant to “Search your knowledge and answer again, focusing on my specific question”
  • Restart conversations when switching topics so the assistant retains focus and accuracy.
  • Use the assistants responsibly—verify their suggestions, and never submit AI-generated work as your own without proper citation.
  • Remember that mastering your own analysis skills is essential for success on the 250-point final project.
  • Share feedback on assistant performance by emailing caltinoz@purdueglobal.edu.

These AI assistants are companions to your learning. Use them thoughtfully, verify their guidance, and build your own expertise.
