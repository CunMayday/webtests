Scoring Logic

Each answer adds weighted points (1-4) to relevant methodologies:

  • High alignment (4 points): Perfect match between answer and methodology
  • Strong alignment (3 points): Very good fit
  • Moderate alignment (2 points): Reasonable fit
  • Weak alignment (1 point): Marginal fit

The methodology with the highest total score becomes the primary recommendation. The system also tracks reasons (why points were added) and cautions (potential concerns).
